Services

Photography of Maternity

These photo sessions are only for expecting mothers. A maternity photography session lasts one to two hours, depending on the package you choose. and can be shot in-studio or outside, depending on your preference. We strive to catch the emotions of moms-to-be, as well as the joy and anticipation of the bundle of sheer delight that is about to bring more happiness into their lives, during a maternity photo shoot.

Maternity portraits are typically taken between the 30th and 35th week of pregnancy. We photograph mom and her baby bump during maternity photography sessions. We shoot a few solo poses of mom, then dad joins her for a few couples poses. Bring any baby items you want photographed, such as your child's first shoes or ultrasound photos.

Newborn Baby Photography

Newborn photography sessions are usually scheduled between four and twelve days after the baby is born. Although we photograph newborns at any age, we strongly advise scheduling a newborn photography session when the baby is less than 12 days old. At this age, they are more likely to sleep for longer periods of time and curl up in womb-like positions. They are also usually fed every two hours. This allows the newborn to sleep for the majority of the session. Our photography studio is fully stocked with a wide range of props, blankets, wraps, outfits, hats, and other accessories. Simply bring your newborn!

Cake smash session

Take advantage of our toddler and children's photography services for candid and authentic photographs of your children having fun. It's the ideal way to capture smiling faces and laughs in photos you'll treasure for a lifetime. Allow your children to be themselves, and we will capture their authenticity and innocence in photos at their best.

Our toddler and children's photography sessions are suitable for any occasion. From a cake smash photography session to commemorate your toddler's first year of life to any and all major holiday photography sessions! A toddler or child's photography session lasts 30-60 minutes, depending on the type of session chosen, and can be done in studio or on location.
